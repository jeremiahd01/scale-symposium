The Moment Is Now

The Future of Defense
Microelectronics
Starts Here

Artificial intelligence is reshaping chip design, fabrication, testing and deployment. Defense microelectronics increasingly demand AI fluency in areas like secure design. Tomorrow’s engineering workforce must be mission-ready from day one, which now means building on traditional areas of study to incorporate cutting-edge tools.

Through SCALE, the only US defense microelectronics public–private–academic partnership, we will explore strategies to prepare future talent and strengthen America’s technological advantage in an era of intelligent systems.
